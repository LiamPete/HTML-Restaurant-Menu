Orgin Story

The owner of H Tai ML, a renowned restaurant, chose to establish their culinary haven in the picturesque town of Zermatt, Switzerland, captivated by the enchanting alpine surroundings and the discerning clientele that frequent this global destination. Zermatt's reputation as a year-round tourist magnet, particularly for its iconic Matterhorn peak and world-class skiing, presented an ideal backdrop for H Tai ML to showcase its distinctive blend of culinary expertise. The owner, an aficionado of alpine elegance, saw an opportunity to introduce a unique dining experience that harmonizes with the town's sophisticated ambiance. By infusing a menu with exquisite flavors and crafting an inviting Alpine Elegance Tavern, the restaurant has become a culinary jewel, attracting locals and visitors alike who seek a gastronomic escape amidst the breathtaking Swiss landscape.
